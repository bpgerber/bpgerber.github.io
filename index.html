Embedded Systems

A variety of different projects done during my time in embedded systems

Cell Printing

A research project for Cardiovascular engineering involving printing cell spheroids using microfluidics. The goal is to create a three dimentional cell culture to test drug penetration with.

REM Monitor

My senior design project. This project had the goal of creating a hardware circuit, PCB, and Android app which monitors and records the participant's sleep cycle. The project features a USB-rechargable battery, over eight hour battery life, detachable enclosure, and sound/visual cues for activating lucid dreaming or smart alarm.

Alcohol Piano

Term project for Embedded Systems. An 8 key piano where the tone of the piano is controlled like a trombone via an anolog potentiometer made with an alcohol sensor. Prototyped with a potentiometer then integrated with the sensor.

About Me

My name is Ben Gerber, I am a senior electrical engineering student at North Dakota State University with an emphasis on biomedical engineering. My preferred area of focus is embedded systems hardware design with some embedded software programming. I am interested in the biomedical industry because it is a way to make a direct, visible impact on peoples' quality of life. The field also presents new challenges for requirements, power consumption, and electromagnetic properties. LinkedIn
